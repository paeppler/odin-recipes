Weisswurst

Ingredients

  • salt
  • 8 white sausages
  • 4 Prezels
  • 4 tsp sweet mustard

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of water with one tsp of salt. Reduce heat and add the sausages. Let them sit for 5 minutes

  2. Remove sausages from water and serve with Prezels and bavarian sweet mustard
